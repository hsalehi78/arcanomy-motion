## 8. The Arcanomy Content Code (Non-Negotiable)

### 8.1 Visual Identity

- **Primary Font:** Bold, ALL CAPS  
  Reason: maximum legibility on mobile

- **Caption Style:** Yellow text + black stroke + glow  
  Reason: survives compression and background variance

- **Keyword Highlighting:** One word per phrase  
  Reason: guided attention, zero visual noise

These rules are functional, not aesthetic.

---

### 8.2 Retention Engineering: The 3–2–1 Rule

**3 Zooms**
- Forces visual novelty
- Prevents static-frame fatigue
- Timed, not random

**2 Overlays**
- One informational (chart or image)
- One emotional (emoji)

**1 Sound Reset**
- Signals transition
- Anchors memory
- Marks payoff moment

Human brains habituate quickly.  
This rule exists to interrupt habituation.

---

## 9. The 20-Minute Daily Execution Loop

### Phase 1: Script (5 minutes)
Structure:
1. Counter-intuitive truth
2. Common mistake
3. Simple fix

Scripts are spoken, short, and non-literary.

---

### Phase 2: Capture (5 minutes)
Rules:
- natural light
- one take
- restart lines without stopping recording

Momentum beats polish.

---

### Phase 3: Build (7 minutes)
Mechanical only:
- auto-captions
- apply preset
- cut silences
- add zooms

No creative decisions allowed.

---

### Phase 4: Polish (3 minutes)
Limits:
- one chart or B-roll
- one sound effect

Polish is capped to prevent scope creep.

---
