
# CapCut Assembly Guide — Arcanomy Reels

## Purpose
This document defines exactly how to assemble Arcanomy reels inside CapCut.
No creative decisions are made here.

## Track Layout
V1: Background
V2: Chart MP4 (if any)
V3: Captions / Overlays
A1: Voice
A2: Music
A3: Sound Resets

## Segment Rules
- 3–4 segments per reel
- 8–15s per segment
- Apply 3 zooms per segment
- Apply 2 overlays per segment
- Apply 1 sound reset at segment start

## Captions
- Use preset X
- One thought per line
- Readable without audio

## Music
- Low arousal
- No vocals
- No drops

## Export
- 9:16
- 1080x1920
